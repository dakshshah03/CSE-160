Grader Notes:
- Performance mode means that reflections update at longer intervals to improve performance (approx every half second). This about doubles FPS
- Aurora adapted from https://www.shadertoy.com/view/XtGGRt.
- Stars/Auroras are animated within the fragment shader.
- Water has reflections from the light of the auroras and from the surrounding environment with a NormalMap overlayed on top.
- Terrain from https://sketchfab.com/3d-models/snowy-mountain-terrain-9fa3c56fd32746bcb0e06cd2c4229ca0
- Helicopter from https://www.cgtrader.com/free-3d-models/aircraft/helicopter/cartoon-low-poly-helicopter
